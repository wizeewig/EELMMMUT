About EEL

The Electrical Engineers' Legation also abbreviated as EEL, is a student body that aims to foster and nurture young talent. The society organizes a plethora of events which includes both technical and non-technical events, thus giving the young and enthusiastic engineers an opportunity to unveil their talents. It focuses at evolving minds and bringing out the CHARISMATIC YOU !!
